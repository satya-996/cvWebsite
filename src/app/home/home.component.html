About me

Hello! I’m Hasika, a Senior at Branham High and Honors Student at West Valley College. With a deep passion for medical sciences, I’m devoted to my academic journey and personal growth.

As the Student Board Trustee for Campbell Union High School District, I advocate for an inclusive educational environment, actively participating in advisory councils. I share joy through music as a part-time piano teacher and ardently champion mental health awareness in our community.

Honored with the President's Volunteer Service Award - Gold and certified by esteemed institutions like Johns Hopkins and the American Heart Association, I look forward to future opportunities to positively impact the community through transparent and collaborative science.
